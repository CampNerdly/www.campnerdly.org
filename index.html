What is it?

Camp Nerdly is a gathering of old and new friends each year to play role-playing and board games and to experience nature. It runs from Friday afternoon to Sunday morning, and we play games together, cook meals in our mess hall, and hang out in cabins. It is cheap (~$60) and a great time!

Camp Nerdly will be held May 16-18, 2014 in the Prince William Forest Park in Triangle, VA. It's about 35 miles south of Washington, DC, and isn't a far drive for most of the East Coast. It's 4-5 hours from NYC, and 4 hours from the Triangle area of North Carolina. Here are directions to the park on Google Maps.
